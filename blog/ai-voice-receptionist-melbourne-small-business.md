## Melbourne small businesses miss 22-62% of calls. Here is what that costs you.

A Melbourne plumber who misses five calls a week is not losing "five calls". They are losing **the five highest-intent enquiries** of their week, because a customer who picks up the phone has already decided they want the problem fixed now.

Australian small businesses miss between 22% and 62% of inbound calls, and home-service operators lose **roughly A$300 to A$1,200 per missed call** when you factor in average job value and lifetime value ([Trillet, 2026](https://www.trillet.ai/blogs/the-true-cost-of-missed-calls-for-australian-small-businesses)). Across a year, that is an average of **A$35,000 in lost revenue** for a single-operator business.

The voicemail fallback does not save you either. 80% of callers sent to voicemail hang up without leaving a message ([B2B HQ](https://b2bhq.com.au/impact-of-missed-business-calls/)), and 60% of Australian callers move straight to the next search result when their call is not picked up ([Ruby AU](https://rubyreceptionist.com.au/blog/missed-calls-australian-businesses/)).

> **The maths most owners never do:** if your average job is worth $500 and you miss two calls a week, you are giving competitors $52,000 a year. Even a modest AI receptionist at $149/month pays for itself with a single saved job per month.

## What an AI voice receptionist actually does

An AI voice receptionist is a natural-sounding software agent that answers your business number, speaks to the caller in plain English, and either books the job, qualifies the lead, or hands the call to you.

It is not a phone tree. There are no "press 1 for bookings" menus. The caller talks, the AI listens, and it responds the way a well-trained front-of-house would.

A typical Amily call looks like this:

- **Answer within 1 ring** with an Australian voice, your business name, and a clear AI disclosure
- **Understand the reason for the call**: new job, existing job, emergency, supplier, or general enquiry
- **Qualify in your industry's language** (e.g. "hot water unit" vs "HWU", "visa class 186" vs "employer-sponsored")
- **Book directly** into your Google Calendar, Cal.com, ServiceM8 or other scheduling tool
- **Escalate emergencies** to your mobile within seconds, with a full SMS transcript
- **Send a confirmation SMS** to the caller and a summary email to you

## Australian-built, Australian-compliant

We built Amily in Melbourne specifically because the generic US AI receptionists do not fit how Australian privacy law works. Here is what we do that most competitors do not:

- **APP 5 notification in the call**, not buried in a privacy policy the caller never reads. Amily says at the start of every call that the caller is speaking with an AI assistant and that the call may be recorded.
- **APP 11 data security**: recordings and transcripts are encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in Australian-hosted infrastructure, per-tenant isolated, and never used to train a general model.
- **Privacy Act 2024 ADM transparency**: the amendment that received royal assent on 10 December 2024 ([MinterEllison](https://www.minterellison.com/articles/privacy-and-other-legislation-amendment-act-2024-now-in-effect)) requires you to disclose automated decision-making in your privacy policy from 10 December 2026. We ship ready-to-use policy copy covering booking, triage and qualification decisions.
- **Victoria Surveillance Devices Act 1999**: a business is a party to calls to its own number, so recording is lawful with disclosure under section 6. Section 11 [restricts downstream sharing of recordings](http://classic.austlii.edu.au/au/legis/vic/consol_act/sda1999210/s11.html) — our architecture prevents that by default.
- **OAIC 2024 AI guidance**: public-facing AI tools must be clearly identified to users. Amily's in-call disclosure plus "powered by AI" branding on caller SMS confirmations implements this by default.

The full details are in our [privacy policy](/privacy.html), and we provide every customer with aligned privacy policy copy for their own site.
